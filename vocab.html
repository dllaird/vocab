    How to Play

    • Click on letters to form words
    • Words must be at least 5 letters long
    • Click "Submit" to check if your word is valid
    • Valid words turn their tiles green
    • Use all 25 tiles to complete the puzzle
    • Click the shuffle button to rearrange tiles
    • Try to complete the puzzle using as few words as possible!

    Tip: The puzzle is always solvable - there's at least one perfect solution!
